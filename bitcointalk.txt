[size=15pt][b]Short intro[/b][/size]

The intention
After visiting Paralelni Polis's Bitcoin-only coffee shop with couple of friends and me being the only person to use Windows Phone I had to ask one of guys to buy drinks for me as there was no Bitcoin Wallet in Windows Phone Store.

Current WP Bitcoin Wallet situation
Here is the list of apps 
Test-net wallet
[url]http://www.windowsphone.com/cs-cz/store/app/bitcoin-wallet/6beb6013-ec4c-47e2-80c8-4b5f9dc4a307[/url]

Why 
[list]
[li]Personal user[/li]
[li]Windows Phone has roughly above 3% market share of Smartphone OS. It is uncomparable to Android's 80% but it is still large portion of cell phones running this OS and community cannot expand without atleast minor support here.[/li]
[/list]
After several days of coding I come up with open-source Blockchain.info API dependant wallet & multipurpose application that has following features:
